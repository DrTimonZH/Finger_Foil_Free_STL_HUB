In this repository, you’ll find STL files ready for you to download, slice yourself, and then print with your own 3D printer. 
These mini foils—think of them like a finger foil, similar to a finger skateboard—let you explore the build and principles of foiling in a playful way. 
Whether in the bathtub or at the lake, assemble your own miniature foil and experience the fun of foiling!
